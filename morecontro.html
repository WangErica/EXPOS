What do Blind Boxes do to us?

We know the saying, "Curiosity kills the cat." But how does this relate to blind boxes? Well, blind boxes stimulates our desire and curiosity. Not knowing, what would be in the box and not knowing if we are the lucky one to get a hidden figure. Humans, like any other animal, are attracted to the unknown. We see most movies and TV shows are based on fictional plots and stories. That is because we are fascinated to things that we have no knowledge of, whether it is the universe or what drink a person got. This curiosity leads many to purchase these blind boxes. The uncovering of what content is in the box generates more curiosity, which leads to more purchases. Our curiosity leads to an addiction, which causes impulsive consumption of blind boxes. The fact that there are always new collections, and series of blind boxes always leaves the customer wanting to see what it will look like. We are never satisfied with we what have, we always like to be up to date and on the top of things. This is the reason why blinds boxes can cause an addiction, one that many fail to notice.
