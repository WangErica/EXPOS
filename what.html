What are Blind Boxes?

Blind boxes is a type of packaging that keeps the inside contents hidden. The inside contents contains a toy figure. This toy figure is part of a collection. The collection is based on a single character. Each collection has a series, or themes, like space, farming, water animals, etc. On the blind box, you would see images of the possible figures in the series that you might get. But there is a hidden figure that isn't displayed on the box. The chances of getting the hidden figure is around 1 in 144 boxes. Which means that is could take up to $1440 until you get the hidden figure. Is it really worth it?
