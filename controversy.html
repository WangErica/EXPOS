Would You Consider it Gambling?

First, what is gambling? Gambling is betting something with value to get an uncertain outcome with the intent to win something else of value. When gambling, you are putting something at risk, in hopes of gaining more that you will lose. So are blind boxes gambling? Yes. Blind boxes are gambling. With the knowledge of knowing that you might not get what you want, you are still making the exchange of money for the figure. When purchasing the blind box, you are hoping that you get the figure you want. This is the same as hoping to win the bet, with the wanted outcome. So how are blind boxes not gambling?
